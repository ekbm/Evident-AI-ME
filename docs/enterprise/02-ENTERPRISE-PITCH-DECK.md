# Evident — Enterprise Solution Overview

**Your AI-Powered Knowledge Assistant**

---

## The Problem

Organisations are drowning in documents. Teams spend hours searching for answers buried in contracts, policies, reports, and compliance documents. Knowledge is scattered across SharePoint, Google Drive, email, and shared drives. Critical information is hard to find, easy to miss, and expensive to get wrong.

---

## The Solution: Evident

Evident is an AI-powered document assistant that lets your team **ask questions and get instant, cited answers** from your organisation's documents — any file type, any department, any language.

### How It Works

1. **Upload or connect** your documents (PDF, Word, Excel, images, audio, video)
2. **Ask questions** in natural language — like talking to an expert who has read everything
3. **Get cited answers** — every response links back to the exact source, so you can verify and trust the information

## Key Capabilities

### For Every Team Member
- **Chat with Evi** — Conversational AI that answers from your documents with full citations
- **Any file type** — PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images (OCR), audio & video (transcription)
- **Smart search** — Ask questions naturally, get precise answers instead of keyword-matching pages of results

- **Knowledge Health dashboard** — See which documents are AI-ready, which need attention
- **Document readiness scoring** — Automated assessment of document quality for AI processing

### For IT & Administration
- **Self-service setup** — Connect external sources through a guided wizard, no developer needed
- **External integrations** — SharePoint, Google Drive, Confluence, Slack, CRM (included in base price)
- **User management** — Admin console with role-based access control
- **Usage analytics** — Monitor adoption, query patterns, and costs

## Security at Every Layer

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest

- **Content protection:** Prompt injection detection, content moderation, source verification
- **Data deletion:** Full deletion on request, including backups
- **Air-gapped option:** On-premise with local AI models (no internet required)

Full security documentation available upon request.
